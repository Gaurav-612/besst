About BESS

A BESS is a group of batteries that stores energy (from the electrical grid or a solar panel system) and automatically adds to your home's energy source when the grid goes down or during on-peak hours.
A Battery Energy Storage system ideally charges and stores power during off-peak and mid-peak hours, and when fully charged, discharges during on-peak hours, making it the home's source of power.
During times of peak electricity demand(for example, when air conditioners ramp up on hot days), many utilities charge customers extra since they have to ramp up additional, often expensive electricity production to meet the demand. These charges are called “time-of-use” or “peak” prices since they are tied solely to when a customer uses the electricity. Energy storage works in similar ways, but since energy storage lets customers decide when to store and when to use the power, they are freed from premium charges, thus increasing cost savings.

About Us

BESST aims to help household owners in making an important decision - whether investing in a BESS is worth it. In accordance with the homeowner’s electricity bill, BESST reduces the burden on them having to research & understand their energy usage. Additionally, by using the Tesla Powerwall as the referenced product, the amount of information needed from them is reduced. BESST essentially presents homeowners with the most important details - including cost savings, reduction in greenhouse gas emissions, and improvement in reliability - that they look for before making this decision.
